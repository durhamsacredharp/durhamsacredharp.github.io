Sacred Harp is an community folk hymn singing tradition from the United States. The Sacred Harp is also the name of the book we sing from.

We sing Sacred Harp in Durham every [dates/times TBC] at [location TBC].

Our singings are open to all. We aren't a choir as we don't rehearse or perform and no-one's in charge. Everyone just sings for their own enjoyment. You don't need to be able to read music yet or have a "good" voice to come along.

The words of most of the songs are from the Christian faith but singers of all religions and none are welcome.
